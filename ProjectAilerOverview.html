Project Ailer Overview

**Project Ailer** is the world’s first hyperscaler designed exclusively for digital media. It combines decades of video expertise with AI-powered automation to solve the unique challenges faced by the digital media industry. **Project Ailer** offers a fully integrated media stack that allows seamless scaling and automation for media companies, from video transcoding to dynamic monetization. The platform provides flexible, usage-based pricing models that scale with demand, and an open ecosystem that facilitates integration with AI services and third-party partners.

With **Project Ailer**, digital media workflows are streamlined, allowing for greater efficiency, reduced costs, and improved outcomes across various media operations. By focusing on media-first cloud services, **Project Ailer** addresses the pressing needs of the industry, bringing a fundamental shift in how media companies approach cloud infrastructure.

---

Market Opportunity

The digital media landscape faces significant challenges with traditional cloud providers that offer generic, one-size-fits-all solutions. Media companies need specialized solutions that can handle the complexity of digital video workflows and scale in real-time.

- The global market for video processing in the media industry is projected to reach **$18.5 billion** by 2024, growing at a **12.8% CAGR** through 2031.

- **Project Ailer** addresses this need by providing a modular, scalable, and AI-driven cloud infrastructure for video processing, transcoding, playout, ad insertion, DRM, and more.

- The platform targets **B2B video services**, enterprises, and organizations that need flexible, efficient, and cost-effective media workflows.

---

Technological Foundation and Services

Developed over the last 10 years by **Noisypeak** in Switzerland, **Project Ailer** is built on a proven, real-world media stack. The platform will offer seven key services at launch:

1. **OTT platform**  

2. **Live transcoding**  

3. **Workflow file transcoding**  

4. **Playout / FAST**  

5. **Server-side ad insertion**  

6. **Real-time repacketizer**  

7. **DRM services**

These core services will be available from day one, with additional services being added over time as the platform expands.

Business Model & Revenue Strategy

**Project Ailer** follows a flexible, adaptive pricing model, which includes:

- **Usage-based pricing**: Scales with customer demand, ensuring businesses pay only for what they use.

- **Revenue-sharing and ad-supported pricing**: Aligned with real business outcomes, offering a variety of monetization options to suit different customer needs.

The platform will also support a modular growth strategy:

- **Stage 1**: Launch with two core services and attract initial customers.

- **Stage 2**: Expand service offerings, targeting a broader customer base.

- **Stage 3**: Introduce additional services and focus on large enterprises, driving global expansion.

AI-powered workflows will drive dynamic content creation, audience engagement, and marketing monetization, further boosting the platform’s value proposition.

### **Formulas for HLS Streaming Pricing (Operator Focused)**

The pricing for **HLS streaming** is dependent on the number of channels, stream resolution, and bandwidth used. Here are the formulas:

1. **Base Fee** for HLS Streaming (dependent on stream resolution and number of channels):

   * **720p**: \$0.10 per channel/day

   * **1080p**: \$0.20 per channel/day

   * **4K**: \$0.50 per channel/day

2. **Bandwidth Costs** (calculated per stream):

   * **720p**: 2Mbps per stream

   * **1080p**: 6Mbps per stream

   * **4K**: 15Mbps per stream

   * Total bandwidth per month = (stream bitrate × number of channels × number of days per month).

3. **Total Monthly Bandwidth Cost**:

   * Example: For **5 channels at 1080p**:

     * Total bandwidth = 6Mbps × 5 channels × 30 days = 9000Mbps/month = 1125MB = 1.125GB per day

     * Monthly total = 1.125GB × 30 days = 33.75GB.

     * If this exceeds the monthly plan's bandwidth limit, overage charges apply.

4. **Additional Charges**:

   * For each additional channel, multiply by the base streaming cost (depending on resolution).

     * For 720p: \$0.10 × 30 days = \$3 per channel/month

     * For 1080p: \$0.20 × 30 days = \$6 per channel/month

     * For 4K: \$0.50 × 30 days = \$15 per channel/month

5. **Multi-Bitrate Encoding**:

   * If enabling multi-bitrate encoding, you can charge a premium for each channel (e.g., +\$8/channel for basic support, +\$20/channel for full support).
